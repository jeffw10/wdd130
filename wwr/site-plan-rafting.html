Rafting Site Plan

Jeffrey Williams

Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote our river rafting business, provide information about rafting tours, and allow online booking.

Audience

Our target audience is primarily young adults who are looking for a fun, safe outdoor adventure. First-time rafters need a website that clearly explains how river rafting works and allows them to book a trip online. Our rafting audience is generally "mobile first" internet users, but the site will also need to function well on desktop and tablet formats.
